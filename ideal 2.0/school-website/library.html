About Our Library

Our library serves as the central hub of academic research, providing students and faculty with a rich selection of textbooks, reference guides, novels, and digital learning devices. It is designed to inspire reading habits and help students perform in-depth research.

  • Air-Conditioned, Quiet Zones
  • Fully Catalogued Online Indexing (OPAC)
  • Daily Newspapers and Weekly Magazines
  • Support for Independent Research Projects

CODE OF CONDUCT

Library Rules & Regulations

To preserve a serene academic atmosphere, students are required to follow these guidelines closely:

  • Strict silence must be observed inside the library at all times.
  • Students must carry their Library ID card for book issuance and entry.
  • Issued books must be returned within 14 days to avoid fine structures.
  • Bags, personal textbooks, and food items are not allowed inside reading stacks.
  • Handling materials with care is mandatory; defacing pages will incur penalty fees.
