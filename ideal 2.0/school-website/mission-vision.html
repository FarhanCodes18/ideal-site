HISTORICAL TIMELINE

24 Years of Legacy

From a small foundation to a recognized CBSE powerhouse, explore our growth timeline.

School Founded (2000)

Ideal Public School was established in Chandori, Waraseoni with a goal to bring premium, values-based CBSE education to the region.

CBSE Affiliation (2005)

Successfully achieved CBSE affiliation, establishing high academic and operational standards.

Campus & Lab Expansion (2015)

Constructed specialized science laboratories, expanded the computer labs, and created a large library facility.

Digital & Smart Classrooms (2020)

Equipped classrooms with digital systems and established an e-library portal for remote learning resources.

Empowering 2500+ Students Today (2026)

Now guiding over 2500 active students and employing 100+ professional staff members, shaping Balaghat's educational future.
