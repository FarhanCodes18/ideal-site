How To Apply

Follow these simple steps to submit your profile for review at Ideal Public School.

1

Verify Eligibility Criteria

Review standard eligibility criteria for academic or administrative positions (degrees, teaching certifications, and specialization experience).

2

Download the Recruitment Form

Download our official **Teacher Recruitment Application Form** PDF using the link below. Print and fill it out completely in block letters.

3

Fill in Details & Prepare Documents

Provide your personal details, academic history, qualifications, and previous teaching experience. Attach copies of your degree certificates and a passport-size photo.

4

Submit the Application

Scan and email the filled application form along with your resume/CV to idealpublicschool47@gmail.com, or submit it physically at the school office reception.

5

Demo Class & Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be contacted by our administration for a scheduled panel interview and a 15-minute demonstration class in their subject field.
