ATHLETIC EXCELLENCE

Physical & Mental Growth

At Ideal Public School, sports are not just extra-curricular — they are an integral component of student life. We foster competitive spirit, sportsmanship, discipline, and physical fitness through daily coaching, inter-school tournaments, and modern playgrounds.

Qualified Coaching

SPORTS DISCIPLINES

Our Sports & Games

Indoor Games Area

Facilities for Chess, Carrom, Gymnastics, and Table Tennis to promote tactical thinking, flexibility, and quick reflexes.

Outdoor Fields

Expansive grassy fields for Football, Cricket pitches, Volleyball courts, and standard Basketball setups with floodlights.

Annual Sports Meet

A flagship annual sporting event featuring house-wise track races, relays, long jumps, shot put, and drills.

Student Achievements

Ideal Public School students consistently win top honors in district athletics, state boxing, and SGFI school games.

Fitness Facilities

Calisthenics areas and dedicated yoga session setups to ensure core physical strength and mental mindfulness.

Running Track

A standard 200m track for sprints, distance running, hurdles, and endurance drills under professional coaches.
