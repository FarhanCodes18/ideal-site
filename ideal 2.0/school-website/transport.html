Safety Measures & Guidelines

To guarantee a secure travel experience, students and parents must respect the following guidelines:

  • Students must arrive at their designated stops 5 minutes before scheduled pickup times.
  • Boarding and deboarding should only be done when the bus is completely stationary.
  • Strict behavior code must be maintained inside the bus; shouting or standing is forbidden.
  • Parents must produce the authorization ID card to receive nursery and primary students.
  • Any change in pickup/drop routes must be reported to the transport desk 48 hours in advance.
